Restaurants

Trattoria Bellavita

Trattoria Bellavita is our Italian restaurant, where you can enjoy authentic Italian cuisine in a cozy and elegant atmosphere. Our menu features a wide range of traditional dishes, including pasta, pizza, and seafood, all made with fresh ingredients and prepared by our skilled chefs.

Dragon Pearl Kitchen

Dragon Pearl Kitchen is our Chinese restaurant, where you can enjoy delicious Cantonese and Sichuan cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our menu features a wide range of traditional dishes, including dim sum, stir-fried vegetables, and signature sauces, all made with fresh ingredients and prepared by our skilled chefs.

Tropic Tide Eatery

Tropic Tide Eatery is our tropical-themed restaurant, where you can enjoy a variety of international dishes in a vibrant and relaxed atmosphere. Our menu features a wide range of flavors, including Caribbean jerk chicken, Hawaiian poke bowls, and fresh seafood, all made with tropical ingredients and prepared by our skilled chefs.

Sol de Lima

Sol de Lima is our Peruvian restaurant, where you can enjoy authentic South American cuisine in a lively and colorful atmosphere. Our menu features a wide range of traditional dishes, including ceviche, lomo saltado, and causa, all made with fresh ingredients and prepared by our skilled chefs.

El Sol Azteca

El Sol Azteca is our Mexican restaurant, where you can enjoy authentic Mesoamerican cuisine in a warm and festive atmosphere. Our menu features a wide range of traditional dishes, including tacos, enchiladas, and tamales, all made with fresh ingredients and prepared by our skilled chefs.

Le Petit Jardin

Le Petit Jardin is our French restaurant, where you can enjoy classic French cuisine in a charming and intimate atmosphere. Our menu features a wide range of traditional dishes, including coq au vin, boeuf Bourguignon, and crème brûlée, all made with fresh ingredients and prepared by our skilled chefs.

Grand Continental Bistro

Grand Continental Bistro is our upscale restaurant, where you can enjoy a variety of international dishes in a sophisticated and elegant atmosphere. Our menu features a wide range of flavors, including Mediterranean mezze, Asian stir-fries, and European classics, all made with fresh ingredients and prepared by our skilled chefs.
